About Festival Planner

Festival Planner is an unofficial Tomorrowland 2026 lineup planner. It helps you rate DJs, mark favorites, and prepare your weekend plan without creating an account.

Privacy-first by design: no tracking, no cookies, no analytics, and no external SDKs.

Local storage only: your ratings and favorites are stored in your browser using IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded.

Import and export: you can export your ratings to a JSON file and import them again later on the same or another device.

Data disclaimer: lineup data can change at any time. Official festival announcements always take priority.
